# Ingredients
6 Mint leaves
3⁄4 oz Simple syrup
3⁄4 oz Fresh lime juice
1 1⁄2 oz White rum
1 1⁄2 oz Club soda/Carbonated water

# Garnish
Mint sprig

# Glass
Highball

# Preparation
Muddle mint in a shaker;
Add syrup, lime juice, rum and ice to the shaker;
Pour shaked well wine into highball glass.
