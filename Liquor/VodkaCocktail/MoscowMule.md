# Ingredients
2 oz Square One Organic Vodka
3 oz Bundaberg Ginger Beer
1/2 oz Fresh lime juice

# Garnish
Lime wheel

# Glass
Moscow Mule mug

# Preparation
Prepare mug with ice cubes and lime wheel garnish;
Add lime juice & giner beer & vodka to the mug.
